Eastern University



Eastern University (Bengali: ইস্টার্ন ইউনিভার্সিটি) or EU is a private university located in Road 6, Block B, Ashulia Model Town, Savar, Dhaka-1345. The Eastern University was established in 2003 under the Private University Act 1992, and later on approved under Private University Act of 2010. The university was set up by Eastern University Foundation - a non-profit, non-political and philanthropic organization.
